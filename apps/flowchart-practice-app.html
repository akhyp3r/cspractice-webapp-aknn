Flowchart Symbol Rules

These are the common symbols you should know for IGCSE Computer Science flowchart questions.

Start / End

Oval or rounded rectangle. Used at the beginning and end of an algorithm.

Process

Rectangle. Used for calculations, assignments and processing.

Input / Output

Parallelogram. Used for input and output.

Decision

Diamond. Used for a condition with branches, such as YES/NO or TRUE/FALSE.

Flow line

Arrow. Shows the direction of control flow.

Connector

Small circle. Used to connect flow lines, especially in larger diagrams.

Flowchart Patterns

Selection

A decision diamond splits into YES/NO branches.

Iteration

A decision can loop back while a condition is true.
